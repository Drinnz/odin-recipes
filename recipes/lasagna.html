Lasagna

Description

A wonderful lasagna made with cheese and minced beef

Ingredients

  • 1 Kg of meat
  • 2 Soup spoon of soy oil
  • 1 Cup chopped onion
  • 2 Garlics
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese

Step by step

  1. In a big pan, put the soy oil and put on the oven for heating
  2. Add onion and garlic. Saute for 2 minutes
  3. Put the meat and mix a lot. Cook for 15 m inutes.
  4. Add pepper of your choice and season with salt. Let it cook for 30 minutes
  5. In a big recipient, add some of the sauce and spread it
  6. Add the lasagna pasta above it
  7. Repeat the process until its done
