Hamburguer

Description

A delicious hamburguer made with handmade meat, with fresh salad and a lot of cheddar.

Ingredients

  • handmade meat
  • lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • cheddar
  • garlic sauce

Step by step

  1. make blends with the meat of your choice
  2. put in the oven for 20 minutes in 180 degrees
  3. prepare the garlic sauce
  4. Cut the bread and put the garlic sauce and the meat
  5. on top of the meat, you'll put the cheddar and the salad of your choice
