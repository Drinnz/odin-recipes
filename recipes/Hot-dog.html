Hot dog

Description

A delicious hot dog with a simple recipe

Ingredients

  • sausage
  • corn
  • hot dog sauce
  • potato sticks
  • ketchup
  • mayonaise

Step by step

  1. Let the sausage cook in hot water for 5 to 10 minutes
  2. prepare the bread with ketchup and mayonaise
  3. put the sausage on the bread and add corn and potato sticks
